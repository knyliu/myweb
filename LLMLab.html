Our Main Goal

Our goal is to train a model that is most suitable for learning the Python programming language.


Why do we want to develop a new model for learning Python?

While learning Python as students, we often asked ChatGPT questions about Python.
However, we're encountering two issues: data sources and divergence.
We can't be certain of the data's origins, and when we wish to delve deeper, we're unsure of where to begin. Regarding divergence, due to ChatGPT's extensive background knowledge, it tends to provide answers that go beyond our expectations, sometimes offering information that is irrelevant to our learning.

What Can Our Model Do?

We hope to be "learner-centered", focusing on learning rather than just obtaining code.
We have clear data sources, providing more reliable and credible information.
The data sources are based on w3schools, and we provide page numbers of the sources, allowing learners to directly study the relevant content.

Reliable Data Sources


This model's data is based on the complete Python tutorial content from w3schools, providing a reliable source of information for generating responses.

Users can also use the responses as a basis to learn more on w3schools.
